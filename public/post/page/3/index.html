Had conversation online with Karin Ahlin, post doctoral researcher at Mittuniversitetet in Östersund. She is researching the societal & economic value of open data and public sector information in Sweden. What Tools have I Used? Asking Good Questions How do I Feel? How do I Feel about the Project Today? I feel that there are a lot of things bubbling around me. With the project I feel that I am starting to connect people in the field that I am researching and discovering. I feel that it is a potential start to build a powerful community and movement for the project. What Surprised Me? How fast I could connect with the people at MetaSolutions AB.
